PS3HEN Installation Guide

Preperation

  • USB Flash Drive with at least 2GB free
  • Any PS3 Model (All phat, slim, and superslim models are compatible)
  • The HFW PUP
  • Internet connection on PC and PS3 (wired recommended, not required)
    •

    Install prep

    Download the PS3HEN 4.89.1 HFW PUP, rename it to PS3UPDAT.PUP

    Grab your USB drive and make sure its FAT32 formatted (Right click > Properties), if not, back up important data and format it as FAT32. Make a new folder on the root of the USB drive called PS3, then inside the PS3 folder, make a UPDATE folder. Put the PS3UPDAT.PUP inside the UPDATE folder.

    HFW installation

    Remove the USB from your PC, and plug it into the PS3 (any port is fine), and navigate to Setting on the XMB. Click on System Update > Update via Storage Media. Once its done checking, go through the prompts and let it install. This will update the console to 4.89 HFW. (HFW Explination here)

    Once the console has rebooted, everything should look the same. In the PS3s browser, navigate to https://ps3addict.github.io/alternate/, if it fails try again. If it fails again, reboot and then try again. After its completed, follow the steps it shows you.

    Finalization

    Once you have finished the install, reboot the console. HEN should now be installed!
